<p><strong>The Portable Image Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sublimation Printer</li><li>Inkjet Printer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable image printer market encompasses two main types: sublimation printers and inkjet printers. Sublimation printers use a heat transfer process, turning solid dye into gas, which permeates special media for vibrant, durable prints, making them ideal for high-quality photos. In contrast, inkjet printers spray liquid ink onto paper, allowing for versatile printing on various materials and producing detailed images, but with potential smudging risks. Both types cater to different consumer needs, from professional photography to on-the-go printing solutions.</p></p>
