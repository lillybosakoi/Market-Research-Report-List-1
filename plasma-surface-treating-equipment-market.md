<p><strong>The Plasma Surface Treating Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Pressure</li><li>Low Pressure / Vacuum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plasma surface treating equipment is categorized into two main types based on pressure conditions: atmospheric pressure and low pressure/vacuum systems. Atmospheric pressure plasma systems operate at ambient conditions, offering efficient surface modification for a variety of materials without the need for a vacuum chamber. In contrast, low pressure or vacuum plasma systems create a controlled environment, allowing for precise treatment of surfaces, enhancing adhesion, wettability, and cleanliness. Both types are widely used in industries such as automotive, electronics, and packaging.</p></p>
