<p><p>The minimally invasive urinary incontinence device market comprises various types, including Tension-Free Vaginal Tape (TVT) and the TVT Obturator System (TVTO). TVT involves placing a mesh tape under the mid-urethra to provide support, effectively reducing leaks. TVTO is a modified version that employs an obturator for less invasive implantation. Other devices include adjustable slings and surgical kits that cater to diverse patient needs. These innovations aim to enhance comfort, reduce recovery time, and improve treatment outcomes for urinary incontinence.</p></p>
