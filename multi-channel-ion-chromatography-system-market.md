<p><p>The Multi-Channel Ion Chromatography System market is divided into two main types: Single Channel and Multi-Channel systems. Single Channel systems analyze one sample at a time, providing high precision and accuracy, making them ideal for specialized applications. In contrast, Multi-Channel systems can simultaneously analyze multiple samples, significantly increasing throughput and efficiency, making them suitable for high-volume testing environments. Both types cater to different customer needs, ranging from research laboratories to industrial applications, depending on the required analysis scale.</p></p>
