<p><p>The Portable Hidden Camera Detection Device market primarily consists of two types: RF-based and LED light gauge-based devices. RF-based detectors identify the radio frequencies emitted by hidden cameras, making them effective for detecting wireless surveillance equipment. In contrast, LED light gauge-based devices utilize specific light wavelengths to reveal hidden cameras by reflecting off their lenses, highlighting their presence. Both technologies cater to security-conscious consumers seeking to ensure privacy in various environments, such as homes, hotels, and offices.</p></p>
