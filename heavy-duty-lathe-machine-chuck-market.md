<p><p>The heavy-duty lathe machine chuck market primarily features two types: the three-jaw universal chuck and the four-jaw independent chuck. The three-jaw universal chuck is designed for quick and easy centering of round workpieces, making it suitable for high-volume production. In contrast, the four-jaw independent chuck allows for precision positioning of irregularly shaped objects, providing enhanced versatility in machining operations. Both types cater to different industry needs, balancing speed and accuracy for various lathe applications.</p></p>
