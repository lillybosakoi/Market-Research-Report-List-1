<p><p>The industrial axial fan market features two primary types: plate type and tube type. Plate type axial fans have a flat design, making them suitable for applications requiring space efficiency and direct airflow. They are commonly used in ventilation systems and industrial processes. Tube type axial fans, on the other hand, are cylindrical and designed for higher airflow and pressure applications, often found in larger facilities such as warehouses and factories where robust ventilation is essential. Both types enhance air circulation and improve operational efficiency.</p></p>
