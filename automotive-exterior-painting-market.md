<p><p>The automotive exterior painting market is segmented into three primary types: ordinary paint, metallic paint, and pearl paint. Ordinary paint offers a cost-effective and straightforward finish, typically available in solid colors. Metallic paint incorporates fine metal flakes, providing a shimmering effect under light and enhancing aesthetic appeal. Pearl paint features reflective pigments that create a multi-dimensional, iridescent look, shifting colors based on the light angle. Each type caters to different consumer preferences, influencing vehicle design and resale value.</p></p>
