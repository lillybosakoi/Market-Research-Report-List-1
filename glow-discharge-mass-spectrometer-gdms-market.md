<p><p>Glow Discharge Mass Spectrometer (GDMS) is an analytical technique used for elemental analysis, particularly in solid samples. The market for GDMS is primarily divided into two types: direct current (dc-GDMS) and radio frequency (rf-GDMS). Dc-GDMS utilizes a continuous discharge, providing stable and straightforward operation, making it ideal for routine applications. In contrast, rf-GDMS employs alternating currents, offering better control of the plasma characteristics, which enhances sensitivity and allows for the analysis of a wider range of materials.</p></p>
