<p><strong>The Stationary Dehumidifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Dehumidifier</li><li>Air Duct Type Dehumidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary dehumidifiers market includes two primary types: conventional dehumidifiers and air duct type dehumidifiers. Conventional dehumidifiers are standalone units designed for individual rooms, efficiently removing moisture from the air to enhance comfort and prevent mold growth. In contrast, air duct type dehumidifiers integrate with existing HVAC systems, providing whole-house humidity control. This type is typically more suitable for larger spaces, offering consistent moisture management throughout the building while maintaining energy efficiency. Both types cater to diverse needs in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
