<p><p>The touchless button market consists of several types, including infrared sensors, capacitive sensors, and other technologies. Infrared sensors detect motion or presence through heat or light signals, making them ideal for applications requiring non-contact activation. Capacitive sensors, on the other hand, use changes in electric fields to sense proximity or touch, offering quick responses and compatibility with various surfaces. Other types may include ultrasonic and optical sensors, each catering to specific industry needs and enhancing user convenience while minimizing contact.</p></p>
