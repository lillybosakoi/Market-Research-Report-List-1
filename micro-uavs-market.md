<p><p>The Micro UAV market is categorized into three main types: Rotary Airfoil, Fixed-Wing, and Others. Rotary Airfoil UAVs, like quadcopters, are characterized by vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, making them versatile for aerial photography and surveillance in confined spaces. Fixed-Wing UAVs resemble traditional aircraft, offering longer flight durations and greater range, ideal for mapping and agricultural monitoring. The "Others" category encompasses specialized designs, such as hybrid UAVs, which combine features of both types for varied operational needs.</p></p>
