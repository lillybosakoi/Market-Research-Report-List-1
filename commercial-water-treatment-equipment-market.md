<p><p>The commercial water treatment equipment market encompasses various types, including filtration, disinfection, adsorption, desalination, and testing. Filtration involves removing impurities using physical barriers, while disinfection employs chemicals or UV light to eliminate pathogens. Adsorption relies on materials like activated carbon to trap contaminants. Desalination is the process of removing salt from seawater to produce freshwater. Testing equipment ensures water quality by analyzing contaminants and adherence to regulations. Collectively, these technologies address diverse water treatment needs across industries.</p></p>
