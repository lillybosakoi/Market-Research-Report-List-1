<p><p>The Medium Unmanned Underwater Vehicle market primarily consists of two types: Autonomous Underwater Vehicles (AUVs) and Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs). AUVs operate independently, carrying out predefined missions for tasks such as underwater mapping and environmental monitoring, thanks to onboard sensors and pre-programmed algorithms. In contrast, ROVs are tethered to a surface vessel and controlled in real-time by operators, making them ideal for tasks requiring human intervention, such as inspection, repair, and complex underwater operations.</p></p>
