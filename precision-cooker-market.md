<p><strong>The Precision Cooker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Immersion Types"</li><li>"Water Bath Types"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The precision cooker market features two primary types: immersion and water bath. Immersion types are compact devices that circulate heated water using a submersible element, allowing for precise temperature control and even cooking of food in vacuum-sealed bags. Water bath types involve larger, dedicated water tanks that maintain stable temperatures, ideal for batch cooking or larger quantities. Both types are popular for sous vide cooking, enabling chefs and home cooks to achieve consistent results with enhanced flavor and texture.</p></p>
