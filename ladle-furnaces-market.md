<p><p>Ladle furnaces are essential in steelmaking for refining and adjusting the chemical composition of molten steel. DC furnaces offer high efficiency, lower energy consumption, and more precise control, making them suitable for high-quality steel production. In contrast, AC furnaces are more commonly used due to their lower initial costs and versatility, accommodating a broader range of steelmaking applications. Both types cater to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
