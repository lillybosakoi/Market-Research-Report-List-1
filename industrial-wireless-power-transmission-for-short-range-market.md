<p><p>Industrial wireless power transmission for short-range applications primarily utilizes two technologies: electromagnetic induction and magnetic field coupling. Electromagnetic induction involves using magnetic fields to transfer energy between coils, ideal for charging small devices and tools without physical connectors. Magnetic field coupling, on the other hand, enhances efficiency by aligning magnetic fields between closely positioned devices, suitable for powering equipment in manufacturing environments. Both technologies streamline operations, reduce wear on connectors, and improve safety by minimizing electrical hazards in industrial settings.</p></p>
