<p><ul><li>Luminescent Devices</li><li>Light-receiving Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical semiconductor devices market comprises two primary types: luminescent devices and light-receiving devices. Luminescent devices, such as light-emitting diodes (LEDs) and laser diodes, emit light when an electric current passes through them, finding applications in displays, lighting, and communication. Light-receiving devices, including photodiodes and phototransistors, detect and convert light signals into electrical signals, enabling usage in sensors, communication systems, and safety devices. Together, these categories drive advancements in optics, enhancing technology across various sectors.</p></p>
