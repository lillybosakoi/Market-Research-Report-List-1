<p><ul><li>Axial Variable Displacement Piston Pump</li><li>Radial Variable Displacement Piston Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Variable Displacement Piston Pump market consists of two main types: Axial and Radial Variable Displacement Piston Pumps. Axial pumps feature pistons aligned parallel to the drive shaft, allowing for compact designs and efficient fluid movement, commonly used in mobile and industrial applications. In contrast, Radial pumps have pistons arranged perpendicular to the drive axis, providing higher displacement per cycle and better performance in high-pressure situations. Both types offer flexibility in flow and pressure control, catering to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
