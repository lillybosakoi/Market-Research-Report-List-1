<p><p>The anesthesia endotracheal tubes market includes two primary types: regular endotracheal tubes and reinforced endotracheal tubes. Regular endotracheal tubes are flexible and commonly used for securing the airway during procedures. They are suitable for various patient populations. Both types are essential for effective airway management.</p></p>
