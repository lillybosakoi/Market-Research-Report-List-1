<p><p>The parallel-shaft gearmotor market is segmented into two main types: DC parallel-shaft gearmotors and AC parallel-shaft gearmotors. DC parallel-shaft gearmotors are favored for applications requiring precise speed control and high torque at low speeds, commonly found in robotics and automotive systems. On the other hand, AC parallel-shaft gearmotors are typically used in industrial applications where durability and continuous operation are essential, such as conveyors and pumps, benefiting from their ability to handle higher power and efficiency.</p></p>
