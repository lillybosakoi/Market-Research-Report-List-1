<p><p>The pellet boiler system market is categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic pellet boilers. Manual pellet boilers require users to manually load pellets and control the heating system, offering simplicity and affordability. Semi-automatic boilers feature mechanisms for easier pellet loading and basic automation, enhancing user convenience. Fully automatic pellet boilers automate the entire process, including pellet delivery and ignition, providing the highest level of convenience and efficiency, making them popular in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
