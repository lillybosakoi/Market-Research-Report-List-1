<p><p>Radial ball bearings are essential components in various machinery, facilitating smooth rotation while minimizing friction. The Conrad type features a simple design with ball retention in a single row, suitable for applications with limited space. In contrast, self-aligning type bearings include a spherical outer race that accommodates alignment errors, enhancing performance in misaligned or vibrating conditions. The market for these bearings is driven by industrial demand, diverse applications in automotive and manufacturing, and the need for reliable, efficient motion solutions.</p></p>
