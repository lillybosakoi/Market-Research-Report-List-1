<p><p>Indoor Positioning and Indoor Navigation (IPIN) systems are technologies enabling precise location tracking and navigation within indoor environments. Proximity-based systems utilize beacons or sensors to provide location data based on nearby devices. WiFi-based systems leverage existing WiFi networks to triangulate positions, offering wider coverage and lower setup costs.
