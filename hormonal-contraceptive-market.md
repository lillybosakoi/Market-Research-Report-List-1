<p><p>The hormonal contraceptive market includes several product types, each offering unique delivery methods and convenience. Oral contraceptive pills are widely used for daily prevention of pregnancy. Injectable birth control provides longer-lasting protection, typically administered every few months. Emergency contraceptive pills are intended for post-intercourse use to prevent pregnancy. Vaginal rings deliver hormones internally and can be replaced monthly, while transdermal patches adhere to the skin, releasing hormones over the week, providing alternative options for women’s reproductive health management.</p></p>
