<p><ul><li>Roof-mounted</li><li>Floor-mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The central inverters market is primarily divided into roof-mounted and floor-mounted types. Roof-mounted central inverters are commonly integrated into photovoltaic (PV) systems on commercial and residential rooftops, optimizing space while converting solar energy efficiently. In contrast, floor-mounted central inverters are typically installed in ground-mounted solar farms, designed for larger-scale energy production. Both configurations serve the same purpose—converting direct current from solar panels into alternating current for grid integration—yet cater to different installation environments and energy demands.</p></p>
